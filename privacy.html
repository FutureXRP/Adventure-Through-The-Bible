Privacy Policy

We built Footsteps for families. That means privacy isn't a legal formality to us — it's a foundational commitment. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We collect the minimum necessary to make the app work well for your family.

Footsteps is operated by Matt and Georgia Blair. We are the "data controller" for the personal information described in this policy.

Contact us: info@biblefootsteps.com

03Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Footsteps is designed for families with children. We take our obligations under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) seriously.

How child profiles work

Children do not create their own accounts. Only adults (18+) may create a Footsteps account. A parent or guardian creates child profiles from within their parent account. By creating a child profile, the parent provides verifiable parental consent for the limited data collection associated with that profile.

What we collect for child profiles

For child profiles, we collect only: a display name (which need not be the child's real name), an optional age, an avatar emoji selection, reading progress (story IDs completed), and badge history. We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information directly from or about children.

Parental rights

As the parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review all data associated with your child's profile at any time
  • Request correction or deletion of your child's data
  • Delete the child profile entirely from your Account page
  • Revoke consent by deleting the child profile or your entire account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@biblefootsteps.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

04How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the Footsteps service
  • Authenticate your account and manage sessions
  • Track reading progress and award badges
  • Process payments and manage subscription status
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, subscription receipts, password reset)
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service, including concurrent session limits

We do not use your data for advertising, do not share it with advertising networks, and do not build behavioral profiles for any commercial purpose.

05Third-Party Services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate Footsteps. Each is chosen carefully:

Supabase (database and authentication) — stores your account data, child profiles, reading progress, and manages authentication sessions. Data is stored on servers in the United States. Supabase's privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

Stripe (payment processing) — handles all payment transactions. We never see or store your full credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

GitHub Pages (web hosting) — serves the Footsteps web application. GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, user agents) as part of hosting. GitHub's privacy policy: GitHub Privacy Statement

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising or behavioral tracking tools.

06Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot see your password
  • Database access is restricted via Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) — each user can only access their own data
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe — we do not store card numbers
  • Session tokens are rotated on each login

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of discovery, as required by applicable law.

07Your Rights and Choices

Access and correction

You can view and update your display name, avatar, and account details from your Account page at any time.

Data deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing info@biblefootsteps.com with the subject line "Delete My Account." We will process your request within 10 business days. Note that billing records are retained for 7 years as required by law, but will be dissociated from your personal information where possible.

Email communications

We send transactional emails only (account confirmation, password reset, subscription receipts). We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in. You can unsubscribe from any email using the link at the bottom of the message.

California residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of the "sale" of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@biblefootsteps.com.

08Cookies and Local Storage

Footsteps uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your session state and reading progress locally on your device. This allows the app to function without requiring a network request for every interaction. We do not use third-party cookies or tracking cookies of any kind.

You can clear your local storage at any time through your browser settings. If you do, your local progress will be lost, though progress synced to your account will be preserved.

09Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (billing records: 7 years).

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Footsteps after the effective date of the revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
