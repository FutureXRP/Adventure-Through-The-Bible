## Suggested priority (biggest narrative gaps first)

1. **The Jacob arc** — Genesis 25–36 (4 stories) closes the only major hole in the Patriarchs.
2. **Exodus core** — The Ten Plagues, The First Passover, Manna, The Golden Calf round out the most-told part of the Old Testament.
3. **Early ministry of Jesus** — Baptism, Temptation, Calling the Disciples, Cana, Calming the Storm.
4. **Revelation & Hope** — adds an entirely new, hopeful closing section (4 stories).
5. **Everything else** — the remaining prophets, Acts episodes, and the "overlooked" gems.
