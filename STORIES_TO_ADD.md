# Stories To Add — Expanded Wishlist Audit

_Audit date: 2026-06-15 · Compared the expanded community catalogue (~140 entries) against the 50 stories currently on the site (`stories.json`, mirrored in `stories-tier2.json`)._

Matches are by **Scripture reference + content**, not title wording (the site uses evocative titles — e.g., "One Stone" = David & Goliath, "The Furnace" = Shadrach/Meshach/Abednego).

## Where things stand

- **On the site now:** 50 stories (both tiers).
- **In the catalogue:** ~140 listed → ~130 unique after removing duplicates/overlaps.
- **Already covered:** ~46 (as their own story, or as the heart of a combined story).
- **Net‑new stories available to add: 77** (listed below).
- **Plus ~20 more** could be created by splitting today's combined stories (see "Splittable").

### The 100 vs. 150 math

| Target | Add | How |
|---|---:|---|
| **100 total** ⭐ _recommended_ | **+50** | Build the 50 ★‑marked stories below (gap‑fillers + most‑loved). |
| **150 total** | **+100** | Build **all 77** net‑new **+** split ~20 combined stories into standalones **+** ~3 bonus. |

> Recommendation: **100**. It closes every major narrative gap and adds the beloved parables/miracles, while keeping the bar high (each story = text for both tiers + narration + word‑timing + quiz). 150 is very doable as a "phase 2" later.
