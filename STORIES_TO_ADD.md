# Footsteps — The 150-Story Master Plan

_Planning doc · 2026-06-15 · This is the target catalogue, not yet built. Today the site has **50** stories (both tiers)._

## Verdict: yes, 150 genuinely great stories is achievable

It comes from three honest sources — no padding:

1. **Keep** the 50 current stories (events stay; a few get unbundled — see below).
2. **Add ~78 new** stories from your catalogue that aren't on the site at all.
3. **Unbundle ~22** stories that the site currently tells as combined arcs (e.g., "The Dreamer's Long Road" = 5 Joseph episodes). Each episode is a famous, tellable story on its own — most children's Bibles tell them separately.

> **50 kept + ~78 new + ~22 unbundled ≈ 150.**
> To land *exactly* on 150 I included your "bonus" picks and **two classics your list missed** (the Transfiguration, Nicodemus), both marked ⟡. The count is easy to tune.

### Honesty notes
- **Unbundling = restructuring, not just adding.** Splitting a combined story means rewriting it into several and **re-generating its audio** (e.g., "Fire from Heaven" → 3 Elijah stories). It's worth it for a 150-story library, but it's real work, not a copy-paste.
- **Weakest links** (fine to cut if you'd rather stop at ~145): Building the Tabernacle, Moses Confronts Pharaoh (overlaps the Plagues), The Coin in the Fish's Mouth.
- **Effort:** ~100 new story *cards* total, each needing Tier-1 text, Tier-2 text (unless flagged), narration audio + word-timings, and a quiz. Best done in phases.
