# Footsteps — Facebook / Instagram Ad Copy Matrix

5 distinct creative **angles** (not tone tweaks — different reasons to click).
For each: 2 Primary Text variants (one short that survives the "See more" fold,
one longer), 3 Headlines (<40 chars), 2 Link Descriptions (<30 chars), and a
recommended CTA button. Character counts are noted so nothing gets truncated.

**Voice:** warm, confident, plain-spoken, never cheesy. Honest claims only — no
fake scarcity, no overpromising.

> ⚠️ **Before you run these:** Angle 5 (and the "Shop Now" CTAs) assume Footsteps
> is a **one-time purchase**, per the brief. The live site currently shows
> "100% free." Make the landing page match the ad before spending, or tell me to
> reframe the paid angle as a free one — it's a 5-minute swap.

Each is retold vividly, with audio narration to listen along. Start at the beginning and walk the whole path.

## How to use this matrix

- **Test angles before tone.** Pick 3 angles (start with 1, 2, and 5), one ad
  each, and let the winners emerge before you polish wording.
- **Pair angle to audience:**
  | Angle | Best audience |
  |---|---|
  | 1 — Whole story in order | Christian parents, homeschool |
  | 2 — Screen time guilt | Christian parents (broad) |
  | 3 — Devotions / Talk About It | Homeschool, kids'-ministry leaders |
  | 4 — Gift | Grandparents / gift-buyers (50+) |
  | 5 — One-time purchase | Broad + subscription-weary parents |
- **Mix and match:** any short primary can run as a Reels/Stories caption; any
  headline doubles as on-image overlay text.
- **Emoji:** at most one, and only where it feels natural (📖). Skip them in the
  gift and one-time-purchase angles to keep them feeling premium.
